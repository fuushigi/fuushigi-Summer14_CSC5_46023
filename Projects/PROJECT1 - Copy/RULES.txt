	RULES TO
WHO WANTS TO BE A MILLIONAIRE

1. Choose Option 1 To Start Game

2. A series of 15 questions will be given.

3. All 15 questions are in multiple-choice,
player must choose A, B, C, D to answer.

4. All quetions must be answered correctly to move on the the next question.

5. Correct answer will grant player a prize of money.

6. There are three check points per money prize, getting a question wrong will
amount to the money prize previous.
